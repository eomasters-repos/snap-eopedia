EOpedia Search

This plugin extends the functionality of SNAP's search box by searching EOpedia.
The search box can be found by default in the upper right corner of SNAP and searches within the available actions and the help pages. This plugin extends this search by the EOpedia Wiki. The term is also looked up in EOpedia and results are listed. When selected the results are shown in the system default browser.

What is EOpedia?

Weren't you searching around and crawling the internet before for a specific bit of information from the EO domain? This happens to me several times even though Wikipedia exists. Wikipedia is intended for the broad audience and not for the EO community. The information I'm looking for is either buried below all other or not contained at all. This is where EOpedia comes into play.
The intention is not to have lengthy full-blown articles but to have articles which provide the most essential information in a nutshell and link to the best resources on the internet.
If you want to take part and help others by sharing your knowledge, request an account.
